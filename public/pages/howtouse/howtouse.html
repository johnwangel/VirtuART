1. Use the Latest Tech ;)

InvisiARt leverages the AR.JS library, which relies on WebRTC, available on iOS 11 and latest Android devices.

2. Allow Access to Camera

Make sure to click 'Allow' when a pop-up asks for your permission to access the camera. To view augmented reality art, you'll need to use your camera.

2. Point Camera at a Printed Marker

InvisiARt relies on pre-registered markers to display the art. You can view the art walls wherever an InvisiARt marker is, or you can print one out and affix to a surface.

3. Add Your Own Mind-Blowing Art

Click on the 'Add Yours' button on the home page to access the paint tool and let your imagination get the better of you.

4. Save to a Common Art Wall

Click 'SAVE' to post your fine artwork to the common art wall. Voila! Aren't you so artsy-fartsy!

5. Check out Your Amazing Art on the Common Art Wall

Your page should automatically refresh to the home screen where you'll view the world through your camera again and see that your stupendous art has been added to the virtual mural! As art walls fill up, they find a new home on the Archived Art Walls page.
