Our App

InvisiARt is our ambitious attempt to provide a commity-generated art canvas through the cutting-edge technology of Aumented Reality. It was created as a final project for the DevLeague bootcamp in Oahu, Hawaii.

Our original inspiration was Pow!Wow!, Honolulu's annual street art festival in Kakaako, during which artists create and present murals painted on the walls of warehouses.

Our app enables "virtual graffiti" - a wall of art that anyone can contribute to from their mobile device and see projected virtually onto any "surface" through Augmented Reality, using the device's camera and special markers.
