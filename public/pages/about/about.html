Our App

InvisiARt is our ambitious attempt to provide a commity-generated art canvas through the cutting-edge technology of Aumented Reality. It was created as a final project for the DevLeague bootcamp in Oahu, Hawaii.

Our inspiration was Pow!Wow!, the annual street art festival in Kakaako, where artists create and present murals painted on the walls in Honolulu's warehouse district.

InvisiARt enables "virtual graffiti" - a wall of art that anyone can contribute to from their mobile device and see projected virtually onto any "surface" through Augmented Reality. Just point your device's camera at one of our special markers, and any space becomes an instant art gallery! See How to Use for more info.
